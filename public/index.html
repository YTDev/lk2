What's PBU ?

The Printify Bulk Uploader is a tool that automates uploading designs to your Printify library using the Printify API token. It simplifies adding multiple designs at once, saving time and enhancing efficiency in managing your design portfolio. By connecting securely with Printify's platform, it streamlines the process of expanding product offerings, making it essential for creators and merchandisers aiming to optimize their workflow.

How it works

Simply input your Printify API token in our secure uploader. This single step ensures a direct connection to your Printify account, bypassing the need for complex authentication.

Choose the design files you wish to upload. Our tool allows for multiple selections, supporting a range of file types for your convenience. Upload your entire collection in one go, saving valuable time.

Once uploaded, your designs are automatically listed in your Printify store. No further action is required, enabling you to swiftly expand your product offerings and reach more customers.
